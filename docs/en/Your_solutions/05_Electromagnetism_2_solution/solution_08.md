# 8. EM Spectrum

## Useful definitions and formulas

In the electromagnetic spectrum:

Shorter wavelength means higher frequency and higher energy.

Increasing wavelength means going from the most energetic radiation to the least energetic radiation.

## Step-by-step solution

The order from shortest wavelength to longest wavelength is:

$$Gamma\ rays \rightarrow X-rays \rightarrow Ultraviolet \rightarrow Infrared \rightarrow Microwaves \rightarrow Radio\ waves$$

## Final answer

Increasing wavelength:

$$Gamma\ rays,\ X-rays,\ Ultraviolet,\ Infrared,\ Microwaves,\ Radio\ waves$$
