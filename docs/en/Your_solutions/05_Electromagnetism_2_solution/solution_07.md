# 7. Wavelength and Frequency

## Useful definitions and formulas

Frequency of light:

$$f=\frac{c}{\lambda}$$

where:

$c=3.0\times10^8\,m/s$  
$\lambda$ — wavelength  

## Step-by-step solution

Given:

$$\lambda=550\,nm$$

Convert nanometers to meters:

$$550\,nm=550\times10^{-9}\,m$$

$$\lambda=5.50\times10^{-7}\,m$$

Now calculate frequency:

$$f=\frac{3.0\times10^8}{5.50\times10^{-7}}$$

$$f=5.45\times10^{14}\,Hz$$

A wavelength of about $550\,nm$ corresponds to green light.

## Final answer

Color:

$$green$$

Frequency:

$$f=5.45\times10^{14}\,Hz$$
