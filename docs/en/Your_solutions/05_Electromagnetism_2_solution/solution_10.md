# 10. Speed of Light in Media

## Useful definitions and formulas

Speed of light in a medium:

$$v=\frac{c}{n}$$

where:

$c=3.0\times10^8\,m/s$  
$n$ — index of refraction  

## Step-by-step solution

Given:

$$n=2.42$$

Now calculate:

$$v=\frac{3.0\times10^8}{2.42}$$

$$v=1.24\times10^8\,m/s$$

## Final answer

$$v=1.24\times10^8\,m/s$$
