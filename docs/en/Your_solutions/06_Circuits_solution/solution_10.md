# 10. Average Current

## Useful definitions and formulas

Average current:

$$I_{avg}=\frac{Q}{t}$$

where:

$Q$ — charge  
$t$ — time  

---

## Step-by-step solution

Given:

$$Q=30\,C$$

$$t=2\,ms=2\times10^{-3}\,s$$

Use the formula:

$$I_{avg}=\frac{30}{2\times10^{-3}}$$

$$I_{avg}=15000\,A$$

---

## Final answer

$$I_{avg}=1.5\times10^4\,A$$

or

$$I_{avg}=15000\,A$$
