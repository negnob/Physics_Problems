# 9. Current

## Useful definitions and formulas

Current is the rate of change of charge:

$$I(t)=\frac{dQ}{dt}$$

where:

$Q$ — charge  
$I$ — current  
$t$ — time  

---

## Step-by-step solution

Given:

$$Q(t)=5t^2+5$$

Differentiate charge with respect to time:

$$I(t)=\frac{d}{dt}(5t^2+5)$$

$$I(t)=10t$$

Now substitute:

$$t=3\,s$$

$$I(3)=10\cdot3$$

$$I(3)=30\,A$$

---

## Final answer

$$I=30\,A$$
