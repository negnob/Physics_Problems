# 11. Power & Energy

## Useful definitions and formulas

Power dissipated by a resistor:

$$P=\frac{V^2}{R}$$

Energy consumed:

$$E=Pt$$

where:

$P$ — power  
$V$ — voltage  
$R$ — resistance  
$t$ — time  

---

## Step-by-step solution

Given:

$$R=100\,\Omega$$

$$V=50\,V$$

Time:

$$t=5\,min=300\,s$$

### Power

$$P=\frac{50^2}{100}$$

$$P=\frac{2500}{100}$$

$$P=25\,W$$

### Energy

$$E=Pt$$

$$E=25\cdot300$$

$$E=7500\,J$$

---

## Final answer

Power:

$$P=25\,W$$

Energy consumed in 5 minutes:

$$E=7500\,J$$
