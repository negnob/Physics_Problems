# 1. Series and Parallel Circuit

## Useful definitions and formulas

### Series connection

For resistors connected in series:

$$R_{eq}=R_1+R_2+R_3$$

Current from the battery:

$$I=\frac{V}{R_{eq}}$$

---

### Parallel connection

For resistors connected in parallel:

$$\frac{1}{R_{eq}}=\frac{1}{R_1}+\frac{1}{R_2}+\frac{1}{R_3}$$

Current from the battery:

$$I=\frac{V}{R_{eq}}$$

where:

$R$ — resistance in ohms $(\Omega)$  
$V$ — voltage in volts $(V)$  
$I$ — current in amperes $(A)$  

---

## Step-by-step solution

Given:

$$R_1=15\,\Omega$$

$$R_2=30\,\Omega$$

$$R_3=50\,\Omega$$

$$V=12\,V$$

---

# Case 1 — Series Connection

### Total equivalent resistance

$$R_{eq}=15+30+50$$

$$R_{eq}=95\,\Omega$$

### Current from the battery

Using Ohm’s Law:

$$I=\frac{12}{95}$$

$$I=0.126\,A$$

---

## Final answer for series connection

Equivalent resistance:

$$R_{eq}=95\,\Omega$$

Current:

$$I=0.126\,A$$

---

# Case 2 — Parallel Connection

### Total equivalent resistance

$$\frac{1}{R_{eq}}=\frac{1}{15}+\frac{1}{30}+\frac{1}{50}$$

Find common denominator:

$$\frac{1}{R_{eq}}=0.0667+0.0333+0.0200$$

$$\frac{1}{R_{eq}}=0.1200$$

Now invert:

$$R_{eq}=\frac{1}{0.1200}$$

$$R_{eq}=8.33\,\Omega$$

### Current from the battery

$$I=\frac{12}{8.33}$$

$$I=1.44\,A$$

---

## Final answer for parallel connection

Equivalent resistance:

$$R_{eq}=8.33\,\Omega$$

Current:

$$I=1.44\,A$$
