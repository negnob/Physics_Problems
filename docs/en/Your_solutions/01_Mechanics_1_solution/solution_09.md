## 9. Momentum Comparison

We compare the momentum of:

- a $2$-gram fly moving at $10\text{ m/s}$
- a $60$-gram tennis ball moving at $1\text{ m/s}$

---

## Useful Definitions and Formulas

Momentum is

$$p=mv$$

Mass must be in kilograms.

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1. Convert masses

Fly:

$$2\text{ g}=0.002\text{ kg}$$

Tennis ball:

$$60\text{ g}=0.060\text{ kg}$$

---

### Step 2. Calculate momenta

Fly:

$$p_{\text{fly}}=0.002\cdot10=0.02\text{ kg m/s}$$

Tennis ball:

$$p_{\text{ball}}=0.060\cdot1=0.06\text{ kg m/s}$$

---

### Step 3. Compare

$$0.06>0.02$$

So the tennis ball has greater momentum.

---

## Final Answer

The tennis ball has greater momentum.

$$\boxed{p_{\text{fly}}=0.02\text{ kg m/s}}$$

$$\boxed{p_{\text{ball}}=0.06\text{ kg m/s}}$$
