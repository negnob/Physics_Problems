## 2. Propagation of Error II

### Useful definitions and formulas

Area of a rectangle:

$$A=L\cdot W$$

For multiplication, relative uncertainties are combined like this:

$$\frac{\Delta A}{A}=\sqrt{\left(\frac{\Delta L}{L}\right)^2+\left(\frac{\Delta W}{W}\right)^2}$$

Then:

$$\Delta A=A\sqrt{\left(\frac{\Delta L}{L}\right)^2+\left(\frac{\Delta W}{W}\right)^2}$$

where:

$L$ — length  
$W$ — width  
$A$ — area  
$\Delta A$ — uncertainty of area  

---

### Step-by-step solution

Given:

$$L=15.3\text{ cm}$$

$$\Delta L=0.1\text{ cm}$$

$$W=8.4\text{ cm}$$

$$\Delta W=0.1\text{ cm}$$

First calculate the area:

$$A=15.3\cdot8.4$$

$$A=128.52\text{ cm}^2$$

Now calculate the uncertainty:

$$\Delta A=128.52\sqrt{\left(\frac{0.1}{15.3}\right)^2+\left(\frac{0.1}{8.4}\right)^2}$$

$$\Delta A\approx1.75\text{ cm}^2$$

Final answer:

$$A=(128.5\pm1.8)\text{ cm}^2$$

So, the area of the plate is about $128.5\text{ cm}^2$, with an uncertainty of about $1.8\text{ cm}^2$.
