## 6. Instrument Precision

### Useful definitions and formulas

For a digital instrument, the uncertainty is often taken as half of the last digit value.

The thermometer reads:

$$25.4^\circ\text{C}$$

The last digit is in tenths of a degree:

$$0.1^\circ\text{C}$$

So the absolute uncertainty is:

$$\Delta T=\frac{0.1}{2}$$

---

### Step-by-step solution

Calculate:

$$\Delta T=0.05^\circ\text{C}$$

Final answer:

$$T=(25.4\pm0.05)^\circ\text{C}$$

So, the absolute uncertainty of the digital thermometer is $0.05^\circ\text{C}$.
