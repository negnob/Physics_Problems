## 5. Percentage Calculation

### Useful definitions and formulas

Percentage uncertainty is calculated by:

$$\text{Percentage uncertainty}=\frac{\Delta t}{t}\cdot100\%$$

where:

$t$ — measured time  
$\Delta t$ — absolute uncertainty  

---

### Step-by-step solution

Given:

$$t=5.45\text{ s}$$

$$\Delta t=0.22\text{ s}$$

Substitute the values:

$$\text{Percentage uncertainty}=\frac{0.22}{5.45}\cdot100\%$$

$$\text{Percentage uncertainty}\approx4.04\%$$

Final answer:

$$\text{Percentage uncertainty}\approx4.0\%$$

So, the percentage uncertainty of the time measurement is about $4.0\%$.
